# Decon-Recon - Another Double Empathy Game

[![Godot](https://img.shields.io/badge/Godot-4.4+-blue.svg)](https://godotengine.org/)
[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-Research-yellow.svg)](LICENSE)

> A simple dialogue-puzzle RPG featuring strategic card-based encounters allowing players to act as mediators in communication scenarios.

## Overview

**Decon-Recon** is an innovative game that addresses the **double empathy problem** - the mutual misunderstandings that occur when individuals from different neurotypes, cultures, or social groups struggle to comprehend each other's perspectives due to inherently different communication frameworks.

The game features a **universal translator and transformer** system that deconstructs texts into neutral core meanings and reconstructs them in various culturally and emotionally diverse representations, promoting enhanced understanding, reduced polarization, and improved communication across different perspectives.

## 🎮 Game Concept

### Core Gameplay Loop

Players become **linguistic diplomats** navigating through campaigns filled with encounters that challenge their ability to interpret and convey critical information across NPCs. The game features two primary encounter types:

#### 1. Deconstruction Encounters
- Players encounter NPCs struggling to express their needs effectively
- Use a **card-based deconstruction system** to remove unnecessary modalities
- Uncover the message's **core meaning** by stripping away cultural, emotional, and contextual layers

#### 2. Reconstruction Encounters
- With the core meaning established, players tailor messages for different NPCs
- Use **submodality cards** collected in their catalogue
- Construct messages that align with the recipient's communication style and needs

### Game Experience

The Letter provides a **DISCOVERY**-driven experience with **FANTASY** and **SUBMISSION** elements:

- **Discovery**: Explore the elasticity of communication, uncover new submodalities, and reveal deeper communication layers
- **Fantasy**: Embody a linguistic diplomat resolving conflicts through language
- **Submission**: Strategic card-based choices within an accessible interface

## 🎯 Core Systems

### Universal Translator and Transformer

The game's core innovation is an LLM-assisted language deconstruction and reconstruction mechanism:

1. **Text Deconstruction**: Uses Large Language Models to peel away cultural nuances, worldviews, perspectives, and emotional sentiments
2. **Core Meaning Extraction**: Identifies the neutral, essential meaning of the original text
3. **Text Reconstruction**: Builds new representations that convey the same meaning but in different cultural, emotional, and contextual frameworks

### Modality System

The game features a sophisticated modality system with categories like:

- **Pragmatic**: Speech acts (requests, offers, promises, commands, warnings, suggestions)
- **Referential**: Spatial contexts (countryside, city, castle, wilderness, coastal, historical)
- **Coherence**: Text structure and flow
- **Emotional**: Sentiment and emotional expression
- **Cultural**: Cultural references and frameworks

Each modality has multiple submodalities represented as collectible cards that players use to deconstruct and reconstruct messages.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Target Experience

The game draws inspiration from mobile games with simple puzzle structure such as hidden-object/connect-3 titles and focuses on:

- **Meaningful Choices**: Every decision affects communication outcomes
- **Strategic Depth**: Card-based mechanics with tactical considerations
- **Educational Value**: Players learn about communication barriers and solutions
- **Accessibility**: Intuitive interface that doesn't require heavy cognitive demands

### Narrative Themes

- Hidden power and knowledge of underappreciated people
- Convincing powerful individuals without the right knowledge or attitude
- Links to arts and philosophy in indirect ways
- Progression from small-scale encounters to larger communication challenges
